Welcome to IDJLM Pro

Here's how to classify your DJ library in 6 steps:

  1. Pick a Folder — point the app at your MP3 collection
  2. Analyse All — extracts BPM, key (Camelot), energy score
  3. Classify All — AI assigns genre + subgenre + confidence
  4. Review — scan the table, edit anything the AI got wrong
  5. Approve ≥80% — bulk-approve high-confidence tracks
  6. Write Tags — saves everything to ID3 tags in your files

Then export to Rekordbox, djay Pro, or M3U playlists.
