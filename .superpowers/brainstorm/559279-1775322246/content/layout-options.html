A — Single Library View

Import controls + pipeline buttons live in a toolbar above the track table. Tracks are always visible. No separate Import or Review tab — it's all one screen. Sidebar shrinks to ~4 items.

Pros

  • See results instantly as you import
  • Pipeline progress visible inline
  • Matches OneTagger's feel

Cons

  • Toolbar gets dense on small screens

  • Progress very visible

C — Guided Steps

A step indicator at the top shows exactly where you are: Import → Analyze → Classify → Review. Each step auto-advances when done. Track table below shows live progress.

Pros

  • Crystal clear what to do next
  • Can't skip steps accidentally

Cons

  • Feels linear — harder to re-run just one step

My recommendation is A — it gives you the OneTagger feel you liked: everything on one screen, no tab-switching to see your tracks. But pick what feels right to you.
